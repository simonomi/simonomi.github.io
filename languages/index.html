alice's languages

this is where i record my progress in learning various (non-programming) languages. the intention is to give other people an estimated level of my proficiency, as well as concrete information regarding what words i know. however, just because a term is listed doesn't necessarily mean that i do know it, just that i should know it.

the lists of terms are created using a script that indexes the obsidian vaults i use (along with the spaced repetition plugin) to study languages. i only run this script occasionally, so terms are typically added in large groups at a time. the newest terms are highlighted in green.
